PRE-ARM

DATES: 15TH TO 18TH OF DECEMBER

In tune with having a golden ARM, it is implied that the Pre-ARM needs to be beyond reproach. The OC is working tirelessly to ensure that all participants will have their expectations met.

The Pre-GA is scheduled from the 15th to 18th of December, with 15th being the arrival day. The pre-ARM will be organized for about 100 medical students all over Africa. About 6 different workshops will run in parallel, during 3 full working days. The focus will be on rural and remote healthcare.

The training sessions will be organized in partnership with the IFMSA International Teams, in addition to team building activities and personalized learning sessions at the Faculty of Pharmacy and Pharmaceutical Sciences building complex on KNUST campus.



Accommodation

Pre-ARM participants will be accommodated in the Engineering guesthouse which is a walking distance to the venue of the Pre-ARM. The rooms are spacious, comfortable and fully air-conditioned. The guesthouse is found in a very serene and beautiful environment with a fully stocked bar.



Transportation

Transport to the Pre-ARM will be provided from Kumasi Airport and from the Asafo bus terminal on arrival day, December, 15th 2018.
