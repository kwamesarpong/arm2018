Word From the chair

Growing up as a child in Ghana, I came across a very thought-provoking quote by an illustrious leader of Ghana and the African Continent, Dr. Kwame Nkrumah, which says “Africa is one Continent, one people and one nation .......”

I have held these words dear in my heart for all my life, as first a pupil in elementary school, then a high school student and even now as a medical student. It is very beautiful to know that this intrinsic passion for our continent is not only unique to me, but also to the lovely members of the organizing committee who have been very committed in every way imaginable in making our bid to host the African Regional Meeting possible.

The decision to host this very unique African event is one that we took wholeheartedly with the aim of fostering the unity and engaging medical students all over Africa in very important conversations about our region to increase our growth and participation in IFMSA. We believe the organization of the ARM in December, as has been done in the past, will afford the continent the opportunity to increase participation of its members during decision-making processes due to the fact that most NMOs on the continent are able to send sizeable delegations during this period of the year.

The very resourceful organizing committee has been working tirelessly around the clock to make available a very conducive and serene environment which we believe will make this annual meeting of medical students on our beloved continent a great success. It is with honour that I humbly welcome our great IFMSA family in Africa to Ghana, the beautiful land of Gold. Thank you in advance for your support in our candidature to host this unique African masterpiece. See you in Ghana! AKWAABA!





Delanyo Amuzu,
Chair





FGMSA

The Federation of Ghana Medical Students’ Association is the association of medical and dental students in Ghana and caters to the thousands of medical students in the country. FGMSA shares in the mission of the IFMSA and offers future physicians the opportunity to start making a difference in the world now.


IFMSA

The International Federation of Medical Students’ Associations (IFMSA), founded in 1951, is one of the world’s oldest and largest student-run organisations. It represents, connects and engages daily with an inspiring and engaging network of 1.3 million medical students from 137 National Member Organisations in 127 countries around the globe.
