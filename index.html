FGMSA-Ghana, representing all medical students of Ghana, is the host of the December Meeting General Assembly 2018 of the International Federation of Medical Students’ Associations (IFMSA).


From December 15th to December 18th 2018, this 3-day conference will create a unique opportunity for innovation and collaboration by gathering 1.000+ medical students and young doctors from over 100 different countries.


This is a once in a lifetime opportunity. What are you waiting for?
