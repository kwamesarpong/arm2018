POST-ARM TRIPS

It would be great pleasure for The Federation of Ghana Medical Students Association(FGMSA) to show our delegates around our wonderful country and its great sceneries through the 2day POST GA TRIP.

The post GA packages are organized in collaboration with other stakeholders of tourism in Ghana in order to provide a marvelous touristic experience.

There are two packages which would be available; the NORTHERN package (Safari Adventure with waterfalls and a monkey sanctuary).

The prices include Full Boarding (accommodation, 3meals per day, transport, POST GA party). Additional activities would be at participants cost.



THE SOUTHERN; PACKAGE 2

This package offers the best of Ghanaian coastline. Ghana's coast is famous for its ancient slave-trading FORTS and CASTLES, beautiful coconut palm shaded beaches, CANOPY PARK WALK, SURFING AND BEACH PARTIES.

The prices include Full Boarding (accommodation, 3meals per day, transport, POST GA party). Additional activities would be at participants cost.
