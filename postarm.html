POST-ARM TRIPS

The Federation of Ghana Medical Students Association(FGMSA) will be showing our delegates around our wonderful country for a period of two days…and we are so excited!! The post ARM packages are organized in collaboration with other tourism stakeholders in Ghana to give you a marvelous touristic experience.

We have available two equally amazing packages:



THE SAFARI PACKAGE

For the adventure-seeker, the experience of a safari trip is an absolute must. Join the Northern safari package and treat yourself with an epic adventure. You get to visit the ancient Larabanga Mosque, be dazzled at the monkey sanctuary, and laugh, eat and relax at the Kintampo Waterfalls.





THE SEASIDE PACKAGE

This package offers the best of the Ghanaian coastline. Ghana's coast is famous for its ancient FORTS and CASTLES. With this package you get the chance to visit these heritage sites to reflect on the Pan-African past (Slave Trade), take a stroll along our beautiful coconut and palm shaded beaches, have a thrilling experience on the canopy park walk and enjoy an ebullient surfing experience and of course, lit beach parties.

The price caters for Full Boarding (accommodation, 3 meals each day, transport, and the POST ARM party). Any additional activities would however be at participants cost.





FGMSA

The Federation of Ghana Medical Students’ Association is the association of medical and dental students in Ghana and caters to the thousands of medical students in the country. FGMSA shares in the mission of the IFMSA and offers future physicians the opportunity to start making a difference in the world now.


IFMSA

The International Federation of Medical Students’ Associations (IFMSA), founded in 1951, is one of the world’s oldest and largest student-run organisations. It represents, connects and engages daily with an inspiring and engaging network of 1.3 million medical students from 137 National Member Organisations in 127 countries around the globe.
