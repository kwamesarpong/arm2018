LOGISTICS

VENUE

This year’s ARM will take place at the Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology, the largest university in the Kumasi Metropolis and in the Ashanti Region and occupies about seven square miles in area. It is located at the outskirts of the city, en route the Kumasi-Accra highway. The university has recreational facilities such as a swimming pool, gymnasium, a university shopping mall and an athletic sports field which could be a perfect getaway after a long day. The University generously offers a large number of conference rooms and well-equipped modern classrooms with functional projectors, speakers, flipcharts and available Wi-Fi services, all to your comfort.

The ARM sessions will be held at the School of Medical Sciences (SMS) which boasts of numerous classrooms with holding capacities ranging from 50 to 200, all equipped with projectors, flip charts and speakers. All the rooms are fully air conditioned and comfortable. There are summer huts, with seating capacities of up to 12, with electric sockets, available for those who prefer to be closer to nature. The Plenary sessions will be held at the School of Medical Sciences (SMS) Lecture theatre which has a seating capacity of over 300, and well equipped with projectors, speakers and amazing lightning.

FOOD

Lunch and supper will be served at a cafeteria at the School of Medical Sciences. Varied options of food will be available, both continental and local, which will be well adapted to suit all backgrounds.



ACCOMMODATION

KNUST Faculty Guest Houses complex

Delegates for the ARM will be hosted at the KNUST Faculty Guest Houses complex, located on the University campus and about 4-minute bus ride to the venue. The participants will be accommodated in very spacious and comfortable rooms which are fully air-conditioned with private bathrooms. The complex features a fully stocked bar and restaurant for interested individuals. Breakfast will be served at the guest houses complex during the ARM.


KINGS TOWER

The rest of the delegates for the ARM will be hosted at the KINGS TOWER HOTEL, an ultra-modern accommodation facility, which is about an 8 minute’s bus ride from the venue. This facility boasts of well-equipped large and comfortable rooms are fully air-conditioned with private bathroom. There’s also a pool and a 24-hour bar and restaurant at the disposal of interested delegates. Breakfast will be served at this facility as well.

NB: THERE WILL BE BUSES AVAILABLE TO TRANSPORT ALL DELEGATES TO THE SESSIONS VENUE RIGHT AFTER BREAKFAST.

FGMSA

The Federation of Ghana Medical Students’ Association is the association of medical and dental students in Ghana and caters to the thousands of medical students in the country. FGMSA shares in the mission of the IFMSA and offers future physicians the opportunity to start making a difference in the world now.


IFMSA

The International Federation of Medical Students’ Associations (IFMSA), founded in 1951, is one of the world’s oldest and largest student-run organisations. It represents, connects and engages daily with an inspiring and engaging network of 1.3 million medical students from 137 National Member Organisations in 127 countries around the globe.
