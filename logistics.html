LOGISTICS

VENUE

This year’s ARM will take place at the Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology, the largest university in the Kumasi Metropolis and in the Ashanti Region and occupies about seven square miles in area. It is located at the outskirts of the city, en route the Kumasi-Accra highway. The university has recreational facilities such as a swimming pool, gymnasium, a university shopping mall and an athletic sports field which are in proximity to the GUSS hostel and could be a perfect getaway after a long day. The University generously offers a large number of conference rooms and well equipped modern classrooms with functional projectors, speakers, flipcharts and available Wi-Fi services, all to your comfort.

The ARM sessions will be held at the Faculty of Pharmacy and Pharmaceutical Sciences building complex on KNUST campus with a holding capacity of up to 300 participants for each classroom. Additional rooms, however, would be available for parallel and side sessions.The Plenary sessions will be held at the University’s most valued edifice, The Great Hall, which has a seating capacity of over a thousand, and well equipped with projectors, speakers and amazing lighting.





FOOD

Varied options of food will be available, both continental and local, comprising breakfast, lunch and supper with coffee and tea breaks in-between sessions, well adapted to suit all backgrounds. Not forgetting a never-ending supply of water and locally made juice throughout the sessions.



ACCOMMODATION

All ARM participants will be housed at the GUSSS hostel complex also on the university campus, about ten-minute walk to the session venue. The rooms are well furnished and have private bathrooms, with each room housing two or three participants. The Hostel has two basketball courts and 2 TV lounges, which can be assessed at all hours of the day. Making the Hostel the ideal place to relax after a hard day’s work.




SOCIAL PROGRAM

UNWIND AND LET LOOSE! Kumasi is a vibrant city of rich culture, beautiful people and hangouts to visit and have fun. After a long day of meetings and discussions, it would be time to let loose, let our hair down and enjoy what the city has to offer. Its quite busy during the day which may be misleading but during the night the city comes alive and we would be right there to explore every bit of it! After all, all work and no play make Jack a dull boy.
