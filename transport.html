TRANSPORTATION

TRAVELLING BY AIR

Kotoka international airport in Accra is the largest airport in Ghana and is the main entry point for arrival. There are domestic flights from Accra to Kumasi with prices ranging from $150-200. Itâ€™s a much faster way to get to the garden city, the venue for the golden event.





AIRLINES

Airlines which operate using Kotoka international airport include: Ethiopian airlines, Kenyan airways, Emirates, Rwandair and South African airways.



TRAVELLING BY BUS

On arrival at the Kotoka international airport, participants have the option of travelling by conventional and comfortable bus coaches. Prices range from $15-20. This provides a cheaper alternative to get to the event as well as enjoy the picturesque landscape.
