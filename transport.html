TRANSPORTATION

Arriving in Ghana

There are four major airports in Ghana, namely, Kotoka international Airport (Accra), Kumasi airport, Tamale airport, Takoradi airport. Participants traveling to Kumasi by air have to use a connecting domestic flight from Accra to Kumasi.


Airlines

International airlines which operate using Kotoka international airport from other African countries include: Ethiopian airlines, Kenyan airways, Emirates, Rwandair and South African airways etc...

Local Airlines which fly from Accra to Kumasi include: Africa World Airlines and Aerogem Aviation, etc


Reaching the Event

The Official pick up point is the KUMASI AIRPORT, which is a domestic airport. Thus, on arrival at the Kotoka international airport (Accra), participants have to connect to Kumasi by air with an estimated travel time of 30 minutes. Another option of special arranged buses from Accra to Kumasi is available only after special request to the OC, the cost of this special arrangement will however be totally covered by participants. These buses connect both cities with an estimated travel time of about 5 hours.


Discounts

OC is working on discounts for both international and domestic flights.









FGMSA

The Federation of Ghana Medical Students’ Association is the association of medical and dental students in Ghana and caters to the thousands of medical students in the country. FGMSA shares in the mission of the IFMSA and offers future physicians the opportunity to start making a difference in the world now.


IFMSA

The International Federation of Medical Students’ Associations (IFMSA), founded in 1951, is one of the world’s oldest and largest student-run organisations. It represents, connects and engages daily with an inspiring and engaging network of 1.3 million medical students from 137 National Member Organisations in 127 countries around the globe.
